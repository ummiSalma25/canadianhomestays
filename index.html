A Trusted Home for Global Learners

Safe, structured, and supported educational experiences abroad.

We facilitate short- and long-term school placements for international students in Canadian public schools (Grades 5 to 12). Our program offers:

  • Full academic integration.
  • Homestay experiences with carefully screened host families.
  • Teacher-led program coordination and monitoring.
  • Immersive English learning and cultural exchange.

Got Questions? we got you

We know this is a big step. That’s why we’ve gathered answers to your most important questions so you feel reassured every step of the way.

What age groups do you accept?

We welcome students from Grade 5 to Grade 12, which includes elementary, middle, and high school levels. We currently do not offer university placements.

Can I choose my host family?

While we can't guarantee specific placements, we carefully match students with screened, caring host families based on lifestyle, interests, and preferences shared in your application.

What support will my child receive while in Canada?

Our certified local coordinators provide ongoing academic, emotional, and practical support, from arrival to graduation.

What are the living arrangements like?

Students live in safe, welcoming homes with Canadian host families, where they receive meals, emotional support, and experience day-to-day family life.

How long can students stay?

We offer short-term and long-term options, including one-semester, full academic year, and multi-year placements.
