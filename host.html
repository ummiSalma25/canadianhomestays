Who Can Become a Host Family?

Whether you're a family, couple, or individual, hosting a student is about creating a safe, welcoming space where they can thrive, learn, and feel at home.

Hosting is available to:

  • Families, couples, or individuals living in a stable home environment
  • Canadian citizens or permanent residents
  • Households willing to provide a private, furnished bedroom and three daily meals
  • People who are open, welcoming, and ready to support a student’s cultural and academic adjustment
  • Note: All host families must complete a background check and home visit.

Important application restrictions apply. See Program & fees for full details.

What Will Be Expected of Me?

As a host, you’ll provide a safe, welcoming space and help your student settle into everyday Canadian life.

  •   Provide a clean, safe, and friendly home environment
  •   Offer a private room with bed, desk, and storage
  •   Supply nutritious meals (3 per day)
  •   Include the student in daily family life
  •   Help them adjust to Canadian culture, school routines, and transit
  •   Communicate regularly with program coordinators
