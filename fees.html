Program Overview

Our international school program welcomes students into Alberta’s inclusive education system, offering academic excellence, language support, and full cultural integration.

●  Full-time, challenging academic studies

●  English language learning support

●  Full integration with Canadian students

●  Preparation for post-secondary pathways

Curriculum & Courses

Students follow the Alberta Education curriculum, ensuring a consistent and recognised learning experience across the province. Core subjects include:

●  English

●  Mathematics

●  Social Studies

●  Sciences

Students may also choose from a wide range of elective courses depending on their school and interests. Learn more about curriculum and options.

Applying for Less than a Semester?

Requests for programming under one semester are approved case by case. Fees are prorated based on annual tuition and insurance. To receive a quote, please contact us.

Payment Information

All fees are payable in Canadian dollars. An invoice will be issued after your application is approved. Payments are processed via Flywire. If you require further assistance with payment by flywire, please refer to their website. Deadline: Payment is due within 30 days of the invoice date.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be provided if the student:

●  Is denied admission to the school

●  Is unable to obtain a Study Permit

  –  Submit the IRCC rejection letter with a Refund Request Form

●  Becomes a Permanent Resident of Canada or obtains a valid Work/Study Permit (program-dependent) prior to September 30

●  Has not yet commenced study with us

Important Notes:

●  No refunds will be issued for late arrivals.

●  Once a student begins study, no refunds will be processed.

●  All refund requests must be in writing and sent for review.

●  Refunds will only be returned to the original payer.

●  No refunds are issued for unforeseen disruptions such as natural disasters, staffing issues, or political events.

How to Apply?

Whether you're a student, a host family, or a parent/guardian, the application process begins with the right form. Download and complete the relevant form below, then submit them via email ( canadianhomestays@hotmail.com) along with any required attachments.

Document Overview

Before you begin your application, please ensure you download and complete the appropriate forms below. These documents are required to process your application and ensure your participation in the Canadian Home Stays programme.

●  Student Application Form: For students applying to participate in the exchange programme (Download Student Application Form)

●  Host Application Form: For families or individuals interested in hosting an internation student (Download Host Application Form)

●  Parental Consent Form: Must be completed and signed by a parent or legal guardian for all applicants under 18 (Download Parental Consent form)

●  Refund Request Form: For applicants requesting a refund due to study permit denial, withdrawal, or ineligibility (Download Refund Request form)
